About PawHub

At PawHub, we believe every pet deserves a loving home. Founded on the principles of compassion and care, we're more than just a pet shop ‚Äì we're a bridge between homeless animals and families ready to welcome them. Our mission is to promote pet adoption, rescue animals in need, and create lasting bonds between pets and people.

Our Mission

Founded in 2015, PawHub was born from a simple belief: every animal deserves love, care, and a safe home. What started as a small group of volunteers has grown into a comprehensive rescue organization serving our community.

We rescue abandoned, stray, and abused animals, providing them with medical care, shelter, and the love they deserve while we search for their perfect forever families. Our goal is not just to find homes, but to create lasting bonds between pets and people.

How It All Began

PawHub was founded in 2015 by Dr. Adrian Pono, a veterinarian who had witnessed far too many animals suffer from neglect and abandonment. What started as a simple mission to save a few stray dogs in her neighborhood quickly grew into something much bigger. Sarah began working with a small group of dedicated volunteers who shared her passion for animal welfare.

In the beginning, operations were run out of Sarah's garage and a rented storage unit. The team rescued 12 animals in their first year, providing medical care, foster homes, and eventually permanent placements. Word spread throughout the community about the compassionate work being done, and soon volunteers, donations, and adoption requests began pouring in.

By 2016, PawHub had grown enough to open its first official shelter facility. What made PawHub different was the holistic approach to rescue ‚Äì not just saving animals, but ensuring they received comprehensive medical care, behavioral rehabilitation, and careful matching with compatible families. The organization also prioritized education, teaching the community about responsible pet ownership, spaying/neutering, and animal welfare.

Today, PawHub operates two shelter locations, a cat sanctuary, and a network of over 100 foster homes. We've rescued and rehomed more than 2,500 animals, and our impact continues to grow. But our mission remains the same as it was on day one: to give every animal the love, care, and second chance they deserve.
